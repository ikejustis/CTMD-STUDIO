Original Soundtrack

closer to my dreams (fanfare)

An original composition and score

Moved by ways of music being able to encapsulate a feeling so little words could describe, this composition was an ode to the journey we embarked on in the pursuit of our dreams. Defined in the corner of my mind during the later stages of my formative years, I beg to question, "how can I express the feeling of the mundane joy felt at the beginnings to the ends of our own direction?"

About CTMD STUDIO

A vision born from a deep, lifelong urge dedicated to bringing the ideas and dreams cultivated in imagination to life. What began as vivid imaginings in adolescence from the cradle of youth, has grown into a profound devotion to this identity, the articulation of this unconscious experience, and the expression of the evocative layers of our world, told through the means of storytelling and visual design. CTMD is the soul of that vision, the story of our dreams, and the tale of our direction.
